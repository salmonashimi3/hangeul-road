# Hangul Road 3D 🚗🚀 — 한글 질주

A 3D Korean-alphabet (Hangul) learning game for kids. Steer your vehicle through the gate with the letter you hear, climb from a baby stroller all the way to a space rocket, and learn consonants, vowels, syllables, and 받침 (final consonants) along the way.

**▶ Play it:** open `index.html` in a browser (needs an internet connection — the 3D engine, Three.js, loads from a CDN).

## Features
- 10 levels, each with a different vehicle and environment: 🏘️ Town → 🎠 Playground → 🏟️ Field → 🛣️ Road → ☁️ Sky → 🚀 Space
- Curriculum: 14 consonants, 10 vowels, 20 common syllables, 받침, 받침 words
- Listen-and-steer gameplay, ⚡ boost button, speed control, hearts & score
- Pick your rider (LEGO-style figures), classical background music per level (toggleable 🎵)
- Level-clear cheer characters (King Sejong, a K-pop star, Admiral Yi Sun-sin), shooting stars & spaceships in space
- Progress saves automatically in the browser

## Controls
- **Steer:** drag / mouse / ← → arrow keys
- **Speed:** ↑ ↓ arrow keys
- **Boost (dash forward):** hold the ⚡ button, tap & hold the vehicle, or hold Space
- 🏠 Home · 🪜 Level Map · 🎵 Music on/off

## How to play / share
This is a single self-contained HTML file — no build step. Just open it, or host it for free with GitHub Pages (see repo settings → Pages).

## Notes
- Vehicle and character designs are original/generic (no real brand names or real public-figure likenesses).
- Pronunciation uses the browser's Korean text-to-speech voice; for best results install a Korean TTS voice on your device.

Made with Three.js. For personal/educational use.
